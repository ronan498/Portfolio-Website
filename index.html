Using a Computer Vision model, specifically for video tracking and classification, I enhance system capabilities to recognize and monitor objects in videos. This technology pinpoints specific entities within a dynamic scene and classifies them into predefined categories. Using a Time Series Forecasting model, I predict future trends and patterns based on historical data. This approach analyzes past sequences to anticipate upcoming events or values.
